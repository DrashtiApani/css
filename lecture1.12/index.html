Meeting

A meeting refers to a gathering with a specific agenda and not just mere gathering of people casually talking to each other.[1] Meetings may occur face-to-face or virtually, as mediated by communications technology, such as a telephone conference call, a skyped conference call or a videoconference. One Merriam-Webster dictionary defines a meeting as "an act or process of coming together" -
